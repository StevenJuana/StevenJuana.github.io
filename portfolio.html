Weather Watcher

Weather Watcher is a weather-based application to help plan out your day through activity suggestion!

    Features Include:
    - Functionality for both current location and specified location through use of APIs
    - Current temperature display (interchangeable between Fahrenheit and Celsius)
    - Activity suggestion based on weather conditions, temperature, and group size
    - Separate menus for activities and food choices
    - Yelp and Amazon integration to search for specific activities in your area

    • Created using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS
    • Available on the Google Play Store!

MyMath

Practice makes Perfect!

MyMath is a learning tool made for students that generates an endless amount of questions and solutions for specific topics in mathematics that a student may need help with. Topics such as addition, subtraction, algebra, and geometry may be practiced until confidence in the topic is reached. For each answer submitted, feedback is given to allows users to improve on their mistakes.

    • Used Flask and Bootstrap as frameworks
    • Coded in Python, Javascript, and HTML/CSS
    • Available for download!
